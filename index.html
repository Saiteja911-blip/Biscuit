The Birth of a Cookie


Long before bakeries filled our streets with sweet aromas, the humble cookie was born by accident. In 7th century Persia, where sugar was first cultivated, bakers began experimenting with small test portions of cake batter to check oven temperatures. These small bites evolved into the first cookies we know today.
